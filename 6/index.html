Intro

The order of words can change the meaning of a sentence.



Similarly, the order of where people look in an image can change the meaning of the image.

Problem Statement

In this project we explore how to change the order of where people look in an image using. We leverage existing deep learning models that predict where people look in an image (DeepGaze) and create a Diffusion based architecture to enduce the average viewre to look towards an area specified by the user.
